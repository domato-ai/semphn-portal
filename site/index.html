How this works.

Plain English · no PHN jargon required
01

See the data

Each step opens with the data we've already pulled for that topic — catchment-level + each of your 10 LGAs. ABS, AIHW, plus what's in your POLAR + rediCASE + CRM. Every figure dated and sourced.

02

Make a decision

Choose which findings rise to the level of a priority for SEMPHN. Talk it through with the assist on the right — ask it to summarise, compare to last year, or suggest framing. Tick what matters.

03

Add the narrative

Write the paragraph that will land in your HNA. The assist can draft a starting point. Save automatically. Mark the step ready when you're done — and the next step opens.
