How it works.

3 steps · ~2 min onboarding

Type what you need

Plain English. "Draft the opening paragraph for the First Nations chapter." Or "Show me CKD prevalence by LGA." The workbench reads your SEMPHN data and answers in context.

Watch it build on the right

As you chat, the canvas on the right of the screen fills in — a paragraph, a chart, a map. Edit any time. Ask the AI to revise, expand, or simplify. Nothing leaves SEMPHN's Azure tenant.

Ship it to PPERS

When every HNA chapter passes the DoH pre-flight, export the artefact bundle. Open ppers.health.gov.au, paste the cheatsheet, lodge. The workbench keeps a versioned record.
