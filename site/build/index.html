Chapter 4 · First Nations people

The South Eastern Melbourne PHN catchment is home to approximately 7,500 First Nations residents. The largest populations sit in Casey (23.4%), Frankston (18.4%) and Mornington Peninsula (17.5%). Median age 25; one in three is under 15.

Socioeconomic determinants

The catchment IRSEO of 25 for First Nations residents is meaningfully above the Victorian average of 14. The lowest IRSEO scores sit in Greater Dandenong, Cranbourne-Narre Warren (Casey), Cardinia and Mornington Peninsula.

Mental health · the standout finding

Mental health is the most common chronic condition reported by SEMPHN First Nations residents. Three LGAs sit above the Victorian average of 18.3%: Port Phillip 23.3%, Frankston 22.0%, Greater Dandenong 21.4%.
